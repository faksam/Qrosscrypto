What Is Bitcoin Wallet? more_vert
What Is Bitcoin Wallet? close

A Bitcoin Wallet is basically the Bitcoin equivalent of a bank account. It allows you to receive bitcoins, store them, and then send them to others. We usually recommend Blockchain Wallet.
